Frequently Asked Questions

Can HealthAssist diagnose illnesses?

No. HealthAssist provides possible conditions based on symptoms, but it is not a substitute for professional medical advice or diagnosis.

Is my personal data secure?

Yes. We take privacy seriously and ensure your symptom inputs are not stored or shared without your consent.

How accurate is the symptom analysis?

HealthAssist uses AI to provide the most likely conditions based on common symptom patterns, but accuracy may vary. Always confirm with a healthcare provider.

Can I use HealthAssist for emergency situations?

No. For emergencies or severe symptoms, contact your doctor or local emergency services immediately.
